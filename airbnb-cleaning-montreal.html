What Is Included in an AirBnb Turnover Cleaning?

Every AirBnb turnover cleaning by Sparkling Stays includes:

  • Full kitchen cleaning: counters, sink, stovetop, microwave exterior, appliance wipe-down
  • Bathroom deep clean: toilet, shower, tub, sink, mirror, floor
  • All bedrooms: beds remade with fresh linens (if provided), surfaces dusted, floors vacuumed/mopped
  • Living areas: dusting, vacuuming, mopping
  • Trash removal throughout the unit
  • Window sills wiped
  • Post-clean inspection and confirmation message to the host

Optional Add-On Services

  • Laundry: wash, dry, and fold linens and towels
  • Restocking toiletries and supplies (you provide the stock)
  • Photo inspection report documenting unit condition
  • Damage reporting and photo documentation
  • Key lockbox pickup and drop-off coordination
