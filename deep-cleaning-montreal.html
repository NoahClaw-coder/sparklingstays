How is deep cleaning different from standard cleaning?

Deep cleaning includes more detailed work and more time in areas that standard recurring visits only maintain.

When should I choose deep cleaning?

Choose it for first visits, neglected spaces, or whenever surface-level maintenance will not be enough.

Can deep cleaning be followed by recurring service?

Yes, that is one of the most common pathways.
