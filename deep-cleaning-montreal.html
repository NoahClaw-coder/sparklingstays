What Is a Deep Cleaning Service?

A deep cleaning — known as a grand ménage in French — is a comprehensive, intensive cleaning that goes beyond the surface level maintenance of a standard clean. While a regular cleaning maintains a home that is already relatively tidy, a deep cleaning is designed to eliminate months of accumulated grime, grease, dust, and buildup from every corner of your home.

At Sparkling Stays, our deep cleaning service in Montreal is our most thorough offering. We recommend it for clients who are:

  • Starting a recurring cleaning plan for the first time
  • Preparing a home for sale or rental
  • Returning from an extended absence
  • Cleaning a property that hasn't been professionally cleaned in 6+ months
  • Hosting an important event and need the home spotless

What Is Included in a Deep Cleaning?

Our Montreal deep cleaning service covers every inch of your home. Here is a complete checklist of what is included:

Kitchen

  • Inside and outside of oven, microwave, and refrigerator
  • Degreasing stovetop, hood fan, and burner grates
  • Inside and outside of all cabinet doors and drawers
  • Countertops, backsplash tile scrubbing
  • Sink scrubbing and drain cleaning
  • Window sills and tracks
  • Floor cleaning including under appliances

Bathrooms

  • Full toilet scrubbing including base and behind
  • Grout scrubbing in shower and tub
  • Shower door tracks and glass de-scaling
  • Vanity, mirror, and cabinet cleaning
  • Ventilation fan cleaning
  • Floor and baseboard scrubbing

All Rooms

  • Dusting all baseboards and crown moulding
  • Cleaning light fixtures and ceiling fans
  • Wiping all doors, frames, and light switches
  • Cleaning windowsills and blinds
  • Moving and cleaning behind furniture
  • Deep vacuuming including under cushions
  • Mopping all hard floors

How Often Should You Book a Deep Clean in Montreal?

Most homes benefit from a deep cleaning every 3 to 6 months. If you are already on a regular bi-weekly cleaning plan with us, we recommend scheduling a deep clean every 6 months — typically in the spring (after a Quebec winter) and fall. For homes with pets, children, or high foot traffic, every 3 months is ideal.

Frequently Asked Questions — Deep Cleaning Montreal

A regular cleaning maintains a home that is already clean, covering surfaces, floors, and visible areas. A deep cleaning is a comprehensive service that includes inside appliances, behind furniture, baseboards, grout scrubbing, and all buildup that accumulates over months. It takes 2–3x longer than a standard clean.

Deep cleaning in Montreal costs between $220 and $450 depending on home size and condition. A 1-bedroom starts at $220, 2-bedroom at $280, and 3-bedroom at $350. We recommend a deep clean every 3–6 months alongside regular maintenance cleaning for best results.

A deep cleaning takes 4 to 8 hours depending on the size and condition of your home. A 1-bedroom apartment takes approximately 4 hours, a 2-bedroom takes 5–6 hours, and a 3-bedroom home takes 6–8 hours. For neglected properties it may take longer — we'll always let you know in advance.

We strongly recommend an initial deep cleaning before starting a recurring plan. This resets your home to a pristine baseline so regular maintenance cleanings can efficiently maintain it.
