Frequently asked questions about deep cleaning in Montreal

How is deep cleaning different from standard house cleaning?

Deep cleaning includes more detail work and more time in areas that routine visits only maintain. It is better for first visits, overdue homes, and spaces with visible buildup.

When should I choose deep cleaning instead of recurring cleaning?

Choose deep cleaning when a home needs a reset first. After that, many clients move to recurring cleaning to maintain the result.
