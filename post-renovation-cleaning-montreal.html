Construction dust, paint residue, adhesive, drywall powder, and debris — professional post-renovation cleaning done right so you can enjoy your newly renovated space. Serving all of Montreal and surrounding areas.

Why Post-Renovation Cleaning Requires a Specialist

Renovations — whether a kitchen update, a basement finish, or a full home gut-renovation — leave behind a type of mess that is fundamentally different from regular household dirt. Construction dust is ultrafine and pervasive. It seeps into vents, settles on every horizontal surface, coats new appliances, and embeds itself in grout lines. Paint overspray lands on windows and fixtures. Drywall compound crusts on floors. Adhesive residue clings to tiles and glass.

Regular cleaning supplies and techniques are simply not equipped for this challenge. A standard mop will smear construction dust rather than remove it. Consumer-grade vacuum cleaners lack the filtration to capture fine particles — they may actually redistribute the dust back into the air.

Sparkling Stays uses professional-grade HEPA-filter vacuums, industrial microfibre systems, and specialized cleaning agents to properly remove post-renovation residue. We know how to clean new surfaces without scratching them, how to remove paint from glass without causing damage, and how to restore grout to its intended colour after a tile installation.

Why Professional Post-Renovation Cleaning Is Worth It

You have invested thousands of dollars in your renovation. Protecting that investment starts with a proper clean. Here is why professional post-renovation cleaning matters:

  • Protects new surfaces: Improper cleaning techniques can scratch new hardwood, cloud new fixtures, or damage delicate finishes. Professionals know which products and methods are safe for each surface.
  • Eliminates health risks: Construction dust — particularly from drywall, plaster, or older buildings — can contain harmful particulates. HEPA filtration is the only way to properly remove them from your air and surfaces.
  • Saves time: Post-renovation cleaning is exhausting and time-consuming. A professional team will complete the job faster and more thoroughly than a DIY effort.
  • Ensures a proper result: You've been living through months of renovation disruption. Let professionals deliver the reveal your new space deserves.

Our Post-Renovation Cleaning Process

Every post-renovation clean with Sparkling Stays follows a structured process designed to achieve the best result:

  1. Assessment: We walk through the renovated space to assess the scope and identify specific challenges (paint on glass, grout staining, heavy dust accumulation, etc.).
  2. Dry phase: We HEPA vacuum every surface from ceiling to floor before any moisture is introduced. This is the most critical step — wetting construction dust before vacuuming it turns it into paste.
  3. Spot treatment: We apply targeted solutions to paint overspray, adhesive residue, grout haze, and other stubborn residue before general surface cleaning.
  4. Wet phase: We wipe down all surfaces, clean fixtures, wash floors, and scrub bathrooms and kitchens.
  5. Detail and polish: Windows, mirrors, new hardware, and fixtures are polished. All protective films and labels are removed.
  6. Final inspection: We do a room-by-room quality check before departure.
