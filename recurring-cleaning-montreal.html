Frequently asked questions

What frequency is most common?

Bi-weekly service is the most common starting point because it balances cost and upkeep well.

Can I change frequency later?

Yes. Many clients adjust after the first few visits once they see how quickly the space gets used.

Do recurring clients need a deep clean first?

Not always, but homes with built-up detail work usually benefit from a deeper first visit.
