What recurring house cleaning usually includes

  • Regular kitchen and bathroom maintenance
  • Dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and routine surface resets
  • Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly scheduling based on your home and lifestyle
  • A clear recommendation if your first visit should start with deep cleaning before ongoing maintenance begins

Who this is best for

Weekly visits

Best for busy family homes, pets, and high-traffic living spaces.

Bi-weekly visits

A common middle ground for apartments, condos, and smaller family homes.

Monthly visits

Useful for lighter maintenance when the home stays relatively controlled between visits.

How recurring cleaning pricing works

Recurring service is usually the most cost-efficient per visit because the home stays easier to maintain over time. Weekly visits cost more often overall but usually less per visit than one-off cleaning.

Frequently asked questions about recurring cleaning in Montreal

What recurring cleaning frequency is most common?

Bi-weekly cleaning is the most common starting point because it balances cost and upkeep well for many Montreal apartments, condos, and family homes.

Can I change my cleaning frequency later?

Yes. Many clients start with one schedule and adjust after a few visits based on how quickly the home gets used.

Do I need a deep clean before starting recurring service?

Not always, but homes with built-up detail work often benefit from a deep cleaning visit before switching to ongoing maintenance.
