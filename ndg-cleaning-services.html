Why NDG Residents Trust Sparkling Stays

Notre-Dame-de-Grâce is one of Montreal's most beloved residential neighbourhoods — a leafy, bilingual community stretching west from Décarie Boulevard to the border of Côte-Saint-Luc. Its streets are lined with classic Montreal triplexes, well-maintained semi-detached homes, and modern condos near the Vendôme and Villa-Maria metro stations. It's a neighbourhood where people put down roots, raise families, and take pride in their homes.

Sparkling Stays has been part of the NDG community for over a decade. We understand the local housing stock: the heritage woodwork that needs gentle care, the older bathrooms that need targeted attention, and the open-concept kitchens in renovated units that require a detailed approach. Our cleaners are trained professionals who treat every NDG home as if it were their own.
