Common questions

What cleaning services do you offer in Montreal?

We offer recurring cleaning, deep cleaning, move-in and move-out cleaning, post-renovation cleaning, commercial cleaning, office cleaning, and Airbnb cleaning.

Do you serve areas outside Montreal?

Yes. We also serve Laval, the South Shore, Brossard, Longueuil, and the West Island. You can check all current coverage on the service areas page.

How do cleaning prices work?

Prices change based on size, condition, frequency, and service type. The pricing page shows common ranges before you request an exact quote.

Where should I start if I am not sure which service I need?

Start with Cleaning Services Montreal for residential needs, Commercial Cleaning Montreal for business spaces, or the Service Areas page if your first question is location coverage.

Can I book online?

Yes. You can book online directly, or request a quote first if the job needs custom scoping.

Should I choose deep cleaning or recurring cleaning?

Choose deep cleaning if the home needs a reset first. Choose recurring cleaning if you want ongoing weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly maintenance.
