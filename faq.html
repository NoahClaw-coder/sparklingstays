Common questions

What cleaning services do you offer?

We focus on recurring residential cleaning, deep cleaning, move-in or move-out cleaning, post-renovation cleaning, commercial cleaning, office cleaning, and Airbnb cleaning.

Do you serve areas outside Montreal?

Yes. This site also includes focused coverage for Laval, the South Shore, Brossard, Longueuil, and the West Island.

How do pricing ranges work?

Pricing ranges reflect the typical size and condition of the space. The final quote changes based on access, frequency, and the level of detail required.

Where should I start if I am not sure which service I need?

Start with Cleaning Services Montreal for residential needs, Commercial Cleaning Montreal for business spaces, or the Service Areas page if your first question is whether we cover your location.

Can I book online?

Yes. You can book online directly, or request a quote first if the job needs custom scoping.
