What Is Included in a Standard House Cleaning?

Our standard house cleaning covers every room in your home with attention to detail that sets us apart from other Montreal cleaning companies. Here is what every regular cleaning includes:

  • Kitchen: countertops, stovetop, sink, exterior of appliances, cabinet fronts, floor
  • Bathrooms: toilet, sink, tub/shower, mirrors, tiles, floor
  • All rooms: dusting surfaces, vacuuming carpets and rugs, mopping hard floors
  • Bedrooms: making beds, dusting furniture, vacuuming
  • Common areas: dusting, vacuuming, wiping switches and handles
  • Emptying trash bins throughout the home
