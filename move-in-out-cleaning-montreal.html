Why Move-Out Cleaning Matters in Montreal

Moving in Quebec comes with unique pressures — especially for the thousands of Montrealers who move on July 1st, Canada's unofficial moving day in the province. Whether you're a tenant trying to recover your security deposit or a landlord preparing a unit for new occupants, a professional move-out cleaning makes all the difference.

Under Quebec's Civil Code, tenants are required to return their unit in the same condition it was received, normal wear and tear excepted. Landlords can withhold part or all of a security deposit if the apartment is not returned clean. A professional clean with documented receipts protects you legally and financially.
