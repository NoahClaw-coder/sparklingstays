Is move cleaning only for empty units?

No. It works for occupied move-in prep, move-out resets, and partially staged spaces too.

How far ahead should I book?

As early as possible, especially near month-end when moving schedules stack up.

Should I choose deep cleaning or move cleaning?

If the main context is a move or turnover deadline, choose move cleaning.
