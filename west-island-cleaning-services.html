Cleaning Services in the West Island of Montreal

Sparkling Stays serves the entire West Island of Montreal — from Pointe-Claire and Kirkland to Dollard-des-Ormeaux, Dorval, and Beaconsfield. Professional, insured, eco-friendly cleaning since 2012. Trusted by West Island families for over a decade, with a 100% satisfaction guarantee.

Professional Cleaning Across the West Island

The West Island of Montreal is a collection of mostly anglophone and bilingual suburban communities stretching along the western tip of the Island of Montreal, from Dorval and Pointe-Claire to Kirkland, Beaconsfield, Baie-D'Urfé, and beyond. It's a region of established family neighbourhoods, well-kept single-family homes, modern condo developments, and spacious properties along the shores of Lac Saint-Louis.

West Island homes tend to be larger than those in central Montreal, and they come with higher expectations for quality. Sparkling Stays has been meeting those expectations since 2012. Whether your home is a 1960s bungalow in DDO, a new build in Kirkland, or a waterfront property in Beaconsfield, our trained and insured cleaners deliver consistent, thorough results every time.
