Cleaning in the Heart of Montreal's Most Vibrant Neighbourhood

The Plateau-Mont-Royal is the cultural heartbeat of Montreal. Bordered by Mont-Royal Park to the north, Rue Sherbrooke to the south, the Mile End to the northwest, and Rosemont to the east, the Plateau is a dense, walkable neighbourhood of iconic Montreal architecture. Its colourful triplexes with exterior staircases, its bustling stretches of Rue Saint-Denis and Boulevard Saint-Laurent, and its beloved Parc La Fontaine make it one of the city's most desirable places to live.

Plateau apartments come with character — and with unique cleaning needs. Heritage hardwood floors, high ceilings with intricate plaster mouldings, exposed brick, and compact kitchens in older units require a cleaner who knows what they're doing. Sparkling Stays has been cleaning Plateau homes since 2012, and our team is fluent in the quirks and charms of this neighbourhood's housing stock.

Cleaning Services for Plateau Homes and Apartments

We offer a complete range of professional cleaning services throughout the Plateau-Mont-Royal:
